Frequently Asked Questions

What is a porta urinal, and when should I rent one?

A porta urinal is a standalone urinal unit designed for high-volume, male-dominated crowds. They are excellent for large public events, concerts, and construction sites to reduce lines and increase efficiency

How do I estimate the number of portable restrooms for my festival?

A good starting point is two portable restrooms for events per 100 guests for the first 3-4 hours. Add one unit per 100 for each additional 3-hour period. Always include ADA-compliant units.

What is the main benefit of a luxury portable toilet over a standard one?

The primary benefit of a luxury portable toilet is the inclusion of a flushing toilet and a handwashing station, which provides a much more comfortable and hygienic user experience similar to a permanent bathroom.

Are fancy porta potties actually different from standard ones?

Yes, a fancy porta potty rental typically offers upgrades like improved exterior and interior styling, better ventilation systems, and often a hands-free hand sanitizer dispenser, providing a noticeably nicer experience.

What makes a deluxe portable toilet suitable for long-term projects?

A deluxe portable toilet rental is built with more robust materials, offers greater interior space, and often includes a non-potable handwashing station, making it more comfortable and durable for weeks or months of use.
